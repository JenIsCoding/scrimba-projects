Top three activities to do in Tuscany

Dive into the art

Galleria degli Uffizi in Florence, the leaning tower in Pisa or Siena's Piazza del Campo? You won't know which one to choose!

Taste Italian food

What about a dinner in a fancy restaurant where you will be delighted by exquisite food? Or are you more of a "trattoria" casual type?

Discover the Tuscan landscape

You have many options: you can spend some days plunged in the countryside (e.g. in Bagno Vignoni, Volterra or San Gimignano) or visiting the Alpi Toscane or, even, relaxing and sunbathing on the beach (e.g. in Calafuria, Viareggio or Follonica)!
